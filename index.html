WikiPathways Academy

Organized training examples for new WikiPathways authors

Follow a New Path

Follow the path of staged tasks below to hone your pathway authoring skills. Each stage links to training challenges. Once you've mastered a given stage, progress to subsequent stages following the path below. Yellow and green stages cover the basic terminology and pathway semantics. Blue and Red stages teach you how to use the pathway editing tools, including the complete modeling of a metabolic pathway. The purple stages introduce you to the WikiPathways website and how to make actual contributions to a crowdsourced collection of pathway information. These skills are prerequisites for moving on to curating existing pathways listed in silver stages.
