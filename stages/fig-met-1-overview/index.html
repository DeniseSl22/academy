Setting Pathway Information Free - Overview

Background

In this quest, you will learn how to convert a published static pathway figure from the scientific literature to an interactive pathway model at WikiPathways. The figure is depicted below and is from the following publication: Linehan WM, Spellman PT, Ricketts CJ, et al. Comprehensive Molecular Characterization of Papillary Renal Cell Carcinoma. The New England journal of medicine. 2016;374(2):135-145.

The pathway depicts parts of the Glycolysis and Krebs cycle pathways. There are some key points about how the pathway is represented in the figure and how this relates to our pathway model:

  • The figure includes specific data overlaid on the pathway nodes (red and blue). For the purposes of a general-purpose pathway model, we should exclude node color.
  • The figure in some cases includes RNA and Protein nodes for the same gene product. We will use only one Gene Product node to represent both protein and RNA forms.
