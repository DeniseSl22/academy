Background

Editing pathway diagrams is a key step in your training to become a pathway curator.

Your Mission

To complete this challenge, click the "Get Challenge" button below. You will receive your challenge as a file named "challenge1". Open it to see your control panel. Inside your control panel, move one of the boxes. Congratulations! You've edited a pathway.
