WikiPathways: Review Outdated Featured Collection Tags

Background

WikiPathways includes a Featured Collection of pathways which contains pathways that represent the highest quality in terms of annotation. A Featured pathway must have a description and must be annotated with ontology tags. The pathway also has to have a complete graph representation, meaning all relevant interactions must be connected to pathway entities and all data nodes must be annotated, at least for gene products. Pathways are added to the Featured Collection by addition of the curation tag "FeaturedPathway", which is revision-specific. The goal of this challenge is to make sure that the Featured Collection is high-quality AND up-to-date with the latest pathway content.

Instructions

  • For the pathway below, follow the link to the Pathway at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways in order to access the editing controls.
  • Review the history of edits since the last tagged revision:
    • If the recent edits are inaccurate or degrade quality, then revert the pathway, and update the faded "Featured pathway" tag by clicking on the click here link in the tag.
    • If recent changes are good, then update the faded tag by clicking on the click here link in the tag.
  • Click "Verify" below to continue.
  • Click "Next" to load the next pathway.
