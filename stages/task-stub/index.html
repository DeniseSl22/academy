WikiPathways: Assess Stub Pathways

Background

Pathways that represent incomplete content that has not been recently edited are tagged with the "Stub" tag. Our goal is to address stub pathways WITHIN 1 YEAR from the date it was first tagged, to avoid accumulating low-quality, incomplete content.

Instructions

  • For the pathway below, follow the link to the Pathway at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways in order to access the editing controls.
  • Perform the following tasks:
  • Review each stub pathway and decide if the content is worth improving. Factors to consider:
    • Is the content original?
    • Is the content at least 50% complete in terms of datanode annotation, connections and layout?
  • If the above criteria are not met, tag the pathway for deletion.
  • If you think the pathway has potential, some options for completing the pathway are:
    • Recruit relevant domain experts to complete the pathway.
    • Contact WikiPathways admins to include the pathway in a curation event.
    • If you have relevant domain knowledge, complete the pathway on your own.
  • Depending on which tags were added or removed for each pathway, click the corresponding "Verify" buttons below.
  • Click "Next" to load the next pathway.
