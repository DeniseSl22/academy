WikiPathways: Review Outdated Curated Collection Tags

Background

WikiPathways includes a Curated Collection of pathways which consist of pathways that are considered to be close to complete in terms of the graph representation. This means that all relevant interactions must be connected to pathway entities, and datanodes must be annotated when possible. Pathways are added to the Curated Collection by addition of the curation tag "AnalysisCollection", which is revision-specific. The goal of this task is to make sure that the Curated Collection is high-quality AND up-to-date with the latest pathway content, since pathways tagged for Curated collection, are being downloaded everyday by researchers for downstream data visualization and analysis.

Instructions

  • For the pathway below, follow the link to the Pathway at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways in order to access the editing controls.
  • Review the history of edits since the last tagged revision:
    • If the recent edits are inaccurate or degrade quality, then revert the pathway, and update the faded "Curation collection" tag by clicking on the click here link in the tag.
    • If recent changes are good, then update the faded tag by clicking on the click here link in the tag.
