WikiPathways: Assess Tutorial Pathways

Background

WikiPathways provides a mechanism for users to try out our pathway authoring tools by creating a Tutorial Pathway. Users can simply add the "Tutorial pathway" curation tag to a newly created pathway, to prevent it from being included in downloaded archives and more generally to inform other users. The goal of this task is to remove tutorial pathways after users have had sufficient time to try out and demonstrate their new skills.

Instructions

  • For the pathway below, follow the link to the Pathway at WikiPathways. You will need to be logged in at WikiPathways in order to access the editing controls.
    • Note: Ignore the Sandbox Pathway (WP4) and all WikiPathways Test, Tutorial and Academy content.
  • Review the pathway history. If the pathway is OVER 30 DAYS OLD, then:
    • Add the "Proposed deletion" tag.
    • Remove the "Tutorial pathway" tag.
    • Click "Verify Tutorial tag removed" and "Verify Deletion tag added" below to continue.
    • Click "Next" to load the next pathway.
